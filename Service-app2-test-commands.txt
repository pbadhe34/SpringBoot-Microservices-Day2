  Update records with curl
 curl -X PUT -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d "{\"id\":\"393e5bfa-e257-4542-b1dc-abbd1bafc733\",\"name\":\"Baba\",\"price\":5.32}  " http://localhost:8084/article-app2/393e5bfa-e257-4542-b1dc-abbd1bafc733
